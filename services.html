Our Service Procedure

Collect Customer Demand Information

We begin by understanding the client’s needs, project scope, and specific requirements.

Suggestion for Design

Our team provides initial design concepts and recommendations based on customer input.

Quotation Communication

We present a detailed quotation with material costs, labor, General Norms and project timelines.

Develop Detailed Design Plans and Renderings

We create comprehensive design plans, including renderings and foundation construction drawings for Tensile Structures.

Signing Contract

Once the design and terms are approved, we formalize the project with a contract.

Manufacturing Process

Our production team manufactures the structure according to the approved design, using high-quality Tensile materials.

Proper Structural Packaging & Operational Delivery

The structure is securely packaged and delivered to the project site, ready for installation.

Installation Process & Testing

Our skilled technicians install the structure, ensuring it is safe, secure, and functional.

Structural Survey for Fabric Making

Before fabric making begins, we conduct a thorough structural survey to ensure precise measurements and alignment.

Fabric Making Process

After installation, fabric is patterned, cut, heat-sealed, tailored, tested for durability, and expertly finished for seamless fitting.

Documentation Work Completion

We ensure all necessary documentation, including warranties and technical specifications, is completed and provided to the client.

Regular After-Sales Maintenance

We offer ongoing support and maintenance to ensure the structure remains in top condition.
