Volley Ball Court Tensile Structure

Volley Ball Court Tensile Structure:

A Volley Ball Court Tensile Structure provides coverage for outdoor volleyball courts, ensuring protection from the elements for players. The tensile fabric allows for quick installation and removal while offering flexibility and durability in varying weather conditions.

FAQ

A Volleyball Court Tensile Structure is a durable roofing system designed to cover volleyball courts, providing protection from sun, rain, and wind. It allows players to play in all weather conditions while maintaining a comfortable environment.

Our tensile structures are made with PVC-coated fabric and galvanized steel frames. These materials are UV-resistant, corrosion-proof, and designed for long-term performance. The finish is lacquered to protect against dirt and weather elements.

Yes, these structures can be customized to accommodate multi-sports facilities, including basketball, badminton, and other court sports. The modular design allows for adaptable layouts.

These structures are used in school playgrounds, college sports facilities, sports complexes, resorts, and recreational clubs etc. where outdoor sports facilities require weather protection.

The translucent material of the structure allows for natural light diffusion, reducing glare on the court surface. Additionally, LED lighting fixtures can be integrated to maintain visibility during night-time play.
